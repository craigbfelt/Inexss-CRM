## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Navigate to your CRM URL (provided by your IT team)
2. Use your assigned email and password
3. You'll see the dashboard with your personalized view

### Your Dashboard

The dashboard shows:
- **Welcome Message**: Personalized greeting
- **Statistics Cards**: Quick overview of meetings, projects, clients, and brands
- **Recent Activity**: Latest actions in the system
- **Quick Actions**: Common tasks you can perform

## User Roles

### Admin (Janine)
- **Full Access**: Create, edit, and delete everything
- **User Management**: Add team members and brand representatives
- **All Reports**: Access to all data and analytics
- **Brand Management**: Set up and manage all 15+ brands

### Staff (Your Team Members)
- **Create & Edit**: Clients, meetings, and projects
- **View All Brands**: Access to all 15 brands
- **Generate Reports**: Create monthly reports
- **Location Based**: Assigned to JHB, Cape Town, or Durban

### Brand Representatives
- **Limited View**: Only see meetings and projects for their brand(s)
- **Reports**: Generate brand-specific reports
- **Read-Only**: Cannot create or edit most data

### Contractors
- **Own Data**: View their own meetings and projects
- **Read-Only**: Cannot create or delete records
- **Client Access**: View client information

### Suppliers
- **Brand-Filtered**: See only projects with their supplied brands
- **Read-Only**: View-only access to maintain data integrity
- **Reports**: Access brand-specific reports

## Managing Clients

### Adding a New Client

1. Click **Clients** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+ Add Client** button
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Name**: Contact person's name
   - **Company**: Architectural firm or company name
   - **Type**: Architect, Developer, Contractor, or Other
   - **Email & Phone**: Contact details
   - **Address**: Full address including city and province
   - **Notes**: Any additional information

4. Click **Add Client**

### Editing a Client

1. Find the client card
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
3. Update information
4. Click **Update Client**

### Searching Clients

- Use the search box to find clients by name or company
- Filter by type using the filter buttons (Architect, Developer, etc.)

## Recording Meetings

### Creating a Meeting

1. Click **Meetings** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Meeting**
3. Fill in details:
   - **Client**: Select the architect/client you met with
   - **Project** (optional): Link to an existing project
   - **Meeting Date**: When the meeting occurred
   - **Location**: Where you met
   - **Attendees**: Who was present
   
4. **Add Brands Discussed**:
   - Click "Add Brand"
   - Select from your 15 brands
   - Check "Required" if the client needs this product
   - Add notes specific to each brand
   - Can discuss multiple brands in one meeting!

5. **Summary**: Overall meeting notes
6. **Action Items**: Add follow-up tasks with due dates
7. **Follow-Up Date**: When to reconnect
8. Click **Create Meeting**

### Why Track Brands Discussed?

This is KEY for your monthly reports! You need to show each brand:
- How many times you discussed them
- How many times they were actually required
- Your "hit rate" - required รท discussed ร 100%

Example:
- Trillidor discussed in 20 meetings
- Required in 15 meetings
- Hit rate: 75%

## Tracking Projects

### Creating a Project

1. Click **Projects** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Project**
3. Fill in:
   - **Project Name**: e.g., "Sandton Office Complex Phase 2"
   - **Client**: Select the architect
   - **Project Number**: Your internal reference
   - **Description**: What's being built
   - **Location**: Project site
   - **Status**: Lead, Quoted, In Progress, etc.
   - **Dates**: Start and expected completion
   - **Estimated Value**: Project worth
   - **Brands Involved**: Which of your 15 brands are in this project

4. Click **Create Project**

### Project Statuses

- **Lead**: Initial contact, potential opportunity
- **Quoted**: You've provided pricing
- **In Progress**: Project is active
- **Completed**: Project finished
- **On Hold**: Temporarily paused
- **Cancelled**: Project won't proceed

## Managing Brands

### Adding Your 15 Brands

1. Click **Brands** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Brand**
3. For each brand add:
   - **Name**: e.g., "Trillidor", "Blinds Direct"
   - **Description**: What they offer
   - **Category**: Product type
   - **Contact Person**: Your rep at the brand
   - **Contact Details**: Email and phone
   - **Website**: Brand website
   - **Notes**: Special information

### Brand Access for Representatives

Once you add brand representatives:
1. Go to **Settings** > **User Management**
2. Create account for the brand rep
3. Assign them access to their specific brand(s)
4. They'll only see meetings/projects for their brands

## Generating Reports

### Monthly Report for All Brands

1. Click **Reports** in the sidebar
2. Click **Monthly Report**
3. Select:
   - **Year**: 2024
   - **Month**: January
4. Click **Generate Report**

The report shows:
- Total meetings this month
- Unique clients met
- Meetings by location (JHB/CT/Durban)
- **For each brand**:
  - Times discussed
  - Times required
  - Hit rate percentage

### Monthly Report for One Brand

1. Follow steps above
2. Select specific brand from dropdown
3. Report filters to show only that brand's data

### Exporting Reports

1. Generate the report
2. Click **Export to PDF** or **Export to Excel**
3. Save for sharing with brand representatives

## Common Questions

### Q: I forgot to record a meeting. Can I add old meetings?
**A**: Yes! When creating a meeting, set the Meeting Date to the past date when it occurred.

### Q: Can I edit a meeting after creating it?
**A**: Yes! Click Edit on any meeting card. Changes are tracked.

### Q: What if I discussed a brand but don't know if it's required yet?
**A**: Leave "Required" unchecked for now. You can edit the meeting later when you know.

### Q: How do I see all meetings with a specific client?
**A**: In Meetings, use the search box to type the client's name.

### Q: Can I delete my mistakes?
**A**: Yes, but only admins and staff can delete. Use carefully!

### Q: The system says I don't have permission. Why?
**A**: Your role (contractor or supplier) has read-only access to protect data integrity. Contact Janine if you need something changed.

### Q: How do I update my password?
**A**: Click your profile picture > Settings > Change Password

### Q: Can I access this on my phone?
**A**: Yes! The CRM is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets.
